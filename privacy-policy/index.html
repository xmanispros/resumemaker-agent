Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

ResumeMaker Agent does not require an account or login. The details you enter in the builder (name, photo, contact information, work history, etc.) are stored locally in your browser's storage so you can resume editing later — we don't transmit this content to our servers to generate your resume.

Advertising

We show a single Google AdSense unit on the Download page before your PDF is generated. Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads; you can review Google's own privacy practices for details on how that data is handled.

Analytics

We may use basic, anonymized analytics to understand which pages are useful and which aren't — this never includes the content of your resume.

Your control

Because your resume data lives in your browser, clearing your browser's site data for ResumeMaker Agent removes it completely. We have no separate copy to delete.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Visit our Contact Us page.
