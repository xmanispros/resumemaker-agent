About ResumeMaker Agent

We believe everyone deserves a professional resume without paying for it.

Our Mission

ResumeMaker Agent was built to solve a simple problem: too many resume builders promise "free" but hide paywalls behind every feature. We created a tool that is genuinely free — no trial that turns into a bill, no card required to download, no dark patterns.

How It Works

Our AI-guided agent walks you through building your resume step by step. It asks for the essential details first — name, contact, and photo — then offers optional sections like summary, skills, education, experience, and projects. You can preview your resume in real time across 90+ templates and download it as a PDF or Word document without creating an account.

Privacy First

Your resume data never leaves your device. Everything is stored in your browser's local storage. We don't collect, store, or transmit your personal information. When you close the browser, your data stays on your device — not on our servers.

Built for Everyone

Whether you're a fresh graduate in India, a professional switching careers, or someone re-entering the workforce — ResumeMaker Agent is designed to work for you. We support multiple template styles (Modern, Professional, Creative, Simple, ATS-Friendly), mobile-friendly editing, and free PDF downloads.
